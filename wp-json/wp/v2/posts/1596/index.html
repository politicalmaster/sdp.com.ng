By Dr. Olu Agunloye, National Chairman, Social Democratic Party, SDP<\/strong>
\n05 April 2021<\/strong><\/p>\n

The news of the demise of our brother and friend, Mr. Yinka Odumakin which occurred on Saturday, April 3, 2021 got to us at the Social Democratic Party, SDP as a rude shock. It was received with pains and disappointment but without questions to the Almighty God.<\/p>\n

The National Chairman, Dr. Olu Agunloye on behalf of the leadership and entire members of the Party (SDP) expresses the heartfelt condolence to his wife, Dr(Mrs) Joe Okei-Odumakin, his children and the rest of his families. We pray that God should grant him eternal rest and comfort his families, friends and associates.<\/p>\n

E-signed:
\nAmbassador Rufus Aiyenigba,<\/strong>
\nNational Press Secretary,
