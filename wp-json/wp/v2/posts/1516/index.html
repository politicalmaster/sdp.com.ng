This is a sad news less than 48 hour that the No1 citizen of the state was tested positive for COVID-19, another forerunner on the battle against the spread of the deadly pestilence was announced death.<\/p>\n

The man worked tirelessly to avert the spread of COVID in Ondo State. His death is another sacrifice for the health of the people. We lost great physician and noble hero in the present of global war.<\/p>\n

While the caused of his death is not traced to the COVID, we lost a father, uncle and friend that matter to all of us, this posed a challenged to us, before death he contributed all his industrious efforts in leveraging our citizens.<\/p>\n

Our sympathy to the Governor HE, Arakunrin Oluwarotimi Akeredolu (SAN), the family of late Dr Adegbenro, All Progressive Party and the good people of Ondo state where he served.<\/p>\n

We should take heart and be strong to continue the fight against the global pandemic as we observe the necessary precautions to prevent the spread of the deadly COVID-19.<\/p>\n

Accept our warm condolence.<\/p>\n

Engr. Gbenga IJI
